Thor

Thor Odinson (a.k.a. "The Mighty Thor") is a fictional character and superhero from Marvel Comics that is based off of the Norse character, as well as the Asgardian ruler of Asgard and the Norse God of thunder, and has appeared as the main protagonist of his titular comics, and a major character in The Avengers comics. He is the son of Odin and Gaea, and the brother of Loki, Baldr, Angela, and many more siblings. He is also a member of the Avengers, the former lover of the mortal known as Jane Foster and the friend/crush of Lady Sif, Hogun, Fandral, and Volstagg.
