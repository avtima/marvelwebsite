Captain America

Captain Steven Grant "Steve" Rogers, also known as Captain America (born July 4, 1918), is one of the main protagonists of the Infinity Saga of the Marvel Cinematic Universe. He serves as the titular protagonist of the Captain America trilogy, one of the titular protagonists of the Avengers tetralogy, a cameo character in Ant-Man, Spider-Man: Homecoming, and Captain Marvel, and a mentioned character in Ant-Man and the Wasp, The Falcon and the Winter Soldier and Black Widow.
